About Us

Cobh Childrens' Theatre is a one stop shop for music, dance , speech and drama club for chidren ages 4-12. Children get to enjoy all their favourite things-Music, Dance, Drama, Games, Activities, Magic, Arts & Crafts, Comedy & Jokes, Kids News-desk & Weather Reporting, Superheroes and Fairies, Fun Masterclasses and SO MUCH MORE in a fun,safe and exciting place. Everything that we offer at Cobh Theatre, is hugely beneficial for a child's well-being. MUSIC - which is proven to ignite developmental and intellectual skills in children. DANCE - great for balance, coordination, as well as physical strength and and flexibility. DRAMA - where chidren enter a world of imagination and where confidence can easily be worked on. These combined with varied and unique games, arts and other offerings make it an easy choice to give your child the gift of COBH CHILDREN'S THEATRE.

Why Do Kids Need Theatre?

Morals

Plays and musicals illustrate many different lessons. For example, Rudolph The Red-Nosed Reindeer The Musical taught lessons about staying true to who you are, as well as lessons of friendship.

Imagination

Creativity

Performing arts teach children how to think creatively through imagination. Creative thinking skills are critical in the world of business leaders, where the ability to create solutions to problems is a necessary and valued asset.

Culture

Through theatre, audiences are immersed in stories about characters from every background imaginable.

Patience

Children may not realise how different live theatre is from sitting in front of a TV or even a movie screen. They will learn how to sit quietly, respect others, and pay attention.

Communication

Theatre exposes young people to new vocabulary and ways of communicating. Trough the arts of dance, acting and music, children learn to communicate in a variety of unique ways.
